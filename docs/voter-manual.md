# Voter Manual

1. Register with full name, email, password, phone, region, and a 10 or 16 digit voter/NID number.
2. Verify the email address.
3. Wait for admin approval.
4. Open the voter dashboard to see next action guidance.
5. Review profile details and update only allowed fields.
6. View available elections and candidate details.
7. Open an active eligible election and select one candidate.
8. Confirm the vote in the modal. A vote cannot be changed after submission.
9. Save the receipt hash and block hash from the success page.
10. Use vote verification to confirm inclusion without exposing identity or candidate choice.
